Mastermind Game

Instructions

Click on the boxes to choose a color. The colors will not repeat unless allow duplicates is checked. Guess the color sequence and check your answer by pressing the "check" button. The small boxes on the right will indicate how accurate your guess is.

Green means a color is correct and in the correct position, light gray means the color is correct but in the wrong position, and brown means the color is not in the code at all. The order of the small boxes do not necessarily correspond to the order of the code--i.e., the first box being green does not necessarily refer to the first color in the sequence being correct.
